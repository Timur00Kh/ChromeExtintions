FAQ:

  • Why some shortcuts don't work?

  • Some shortcuts could be in use by other extensions.
    Check browser extension shortcuts, other browsers and programs on your computer.

    Also it could be keyboard layout inconsistency.

  • Why HTML5 Video Shortcutter don't work with some videos?
  • It isn't HTML5 video.
  • Video wasn't focused.

    HTML5 Video Shortcutter interacts with last clicked video or with first located video.

  • Video was loaded after the page.

    Sometimes videos loading after the page, so you should to reload script
